IWU Bacteriophages

Welcome to the IWU Bacteriophages Database, where phages discovered by Illinois Wesleyan University students are stored for future research. Currently, students are working to isolate newly discovered phages from several strains of bacteria, including strains from Rhodobacter capsulatus and Mycobacterium smegmatis.

The Database

This website provides students with user-friendly access to the Illinois Wesleyan University database. Here, students can upload information pertaining to the discovery of their own newly found phages. Students must first have an account, which is provided to them by their lab advisor to prevent information from being stolen. Students can then add, view, modify, and delete phages with approval from an admin user.
